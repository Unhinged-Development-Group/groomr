## Quick reference

- **Brand voice:** warm, direct, quietly confident, dog-obsessed. British English. No emoji. One pun max.
- **Tagline:** *"Your dog deserves a regular."* — always quoted, italics, full stop.
- **Colours:** Alabaster Cream `#f9f8f4` canvas, Deep Slate `#2c3e50` text, Groomr Gold `#eae45c` for primary CTAs only (never text), Sage Leaf `#88a096`, Pebble Grey `#95a5a6`, Muted Terracotta `#c87964` (CTA hover).
- **Type:** Fredoka 500/700 for H1/H2 (Title Case). Nunito 400/700/800 for everything else.
- **Buttons:** fully pill-shaped, Gold→Terracotta on hover at 0.3s ease.
- **Icons:** offset coloured "splash" circle (Gold or Terracotta @ 0.55) + 2px Deep Slate stroke glyph at 24-px viewBox.
- **Footer:** Deep Slate with 8px Groomr Gold top accent border. Always.
