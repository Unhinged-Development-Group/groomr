1. About this Policy

This policy sets out the commission incentives and onboarding packages available to Groomers who register on the Groomr platform. It applies to all Groomer accounts from the date of registration.

This policy should be read alongside the Groomer Terms, which govern your use of the platform more broadly, and the Platform Terms of Use.

The incentives described in this policy are in addition to your standard rights and obligations under the Groomer Terms. Nothing in this policy limits those rights.

2. Standard Sign-Up Incentive

2.1 0% commission on your first 150 bookings

Every Groomer who registers on Groomr receives 0% platform commission on their first 150 completed bookings. This applies to all Groomers regardless of when they join — it is a permanent, always-on feature of the Groomr platform.

No action is required to claim this incentive. It is applied automatically to your account from the moment of registration.

2.2 Transition to standard commission

Once your 150th completed booking has been processed, the standard Groomr commission rate of 8% will apply to all subsequent bookings. You will receive a notification when you are approaching this threshold so you can plan accordingly.

2.3 What counts as a completed booking

For the purposes of counting towards your 150-booking incentive, a completed booking is one where:

  • the appointment has taken place and been marked as completed on the platform; and
  • payment has been successfully processed and is not subject to an active dispute or chargeback.

Bookings that are cancelled, result in a no-show, or are subject to a full refund do not count towards the 150-booking total. Partially refunded bookings count as completed for the purpose of this incentive.

3. Founding Groomer Status

3.1 Eligibility

Groomers who complete their registration on the Groomr platform within the first three months of Groomr's public launch are designated Founding Groomers. The Founding Groomer window runs from Groomr's launch date to [LAUNCH DATE + 3 MONTHS].

Founding Groomer status is granted automatically upon registration within the eligibility window. It is permanent — once earned, it is not affected by subsequent changes to the platform or the closure and re-opening of your account.

3.2 What Founding Groomer status includes

In addition to the standard sign-up incentive (section 2), Founding Groomers are eligible to purchase the Founding Groomer Onboarding Package described in section 4. This package is exclusively available to Groomers who hold Founding Groomer status and cannot be purchased by Groomers who register after the eligibility window closes.

Founding Groomers also receive the standard 0% commission incentive on their first 150 completed bookings as described in section 2.

4. Founding Groomer Onboarding Package

4.1 Overview

The Founding Groomer Onboarding Package is a fully personal, done-for-you onboarding service delivered by Andrew Hughes (Groomr founder). It is designed to get Founding Groomers fully set up, verified, and operational on the platform with zero friction — handling all of the technical and administrative setup on your behalf.

The package is available for a one-off fee of £49.

4.2 What's included

The package covers everything required to go live on Groomr and begin taking bookings:

  • Client set-up and migration — your existing client list is migrated to Groomr. We work with you to export contacts from wherever you currently hold them (WhatsApp groups, spreadsheets, notes, paper records) and send personalised invite links so your regulars can find and book you on the platform from day one.
  • Complete profile creation — your public Groomr profile is built from scratch, including your bio, profile and banner images, and any other content that represents your business.
  • Service listings, pricing and availability — all of your services are set up with accurate descriptions, durations, and pricing. Your working hours, availability, buffer times, and any blocked dates are configured so your calendar is accurate and ready to accept bookings.
  • Fast-track verification — your verification documents are reviewed and processed as a priority, getting your Verified badge applied ahead of the standard queue.
  • Payout setup — your Stripe Connect account is set up and linked to your Groomr profile so you can receive payouts from day one. We walk through this with you to ensure everything is connected correctly.
  • In-person platform demonstration — a personal walkthrough of the Groomr platform so you understand how bookings work, how clients experience the booking flow, how to manage your dashboard, and how to handle messaging and appointments.
  • Direct support during early adoption — direct access to Andrew during your first weeks on the platform. Any questions, issues, or changes needed are handled personally and promptly.

4.3 Pricing and how to book

The package is available to Founding Groomers at a one-off price of £49. To book your onboarding session, contact us at hello@groomr.uk with the subject line "Founding Groomer Onboarding". We will confirm your Founding Groomer eligibility, arrange payment, and schedule your session.

The £49 fee is payable prior to your onboarding session. Payment details will be provided on confirmation.

4.4 Delivery and availability

Onboarding sessions are delivered in person where possible, with remote delivery available where geography makes in-person attendance impractical. Sessions are conducted by Andrew Hughes personally.

Availability for onboarding sessions is limited. Sessions are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. We will provide an estimated date when confirming your booking.

The Founding Groomer Onboarding Package is only available to Groomers who registered during the Founding Groomer eligibility window. It cannot be purchased after that window closes, regardless of when you book your session.

5. General Terms

5.1 Automatic application

The standard sign-up incentive (section 2) is applied automatically. There is nothing you need to do to activate it. Your booking count is tracked by the platform and visible in your Groomer dashboard.

5.2 Non-transferability

Commission incentives and Founding Groomer status are tied to your individual Groomer account. They cannot be transferred to another account, sold, gifted, or otherwise assigned to another person or business.

5.3 Onboarding package refunds

If you have purchased the Founding Groomer Onboarding Package and wish to cancel before your session has begun, please contact us at hello@groomr.uk. Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. Once an onboarding session has commenced, the fee is non-refundable.

5.4 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. However:

  • your 150-booking commission-free count will never be reduced once your account has been registered;
  • Founding Groomer status, once granted, will not be revoked as a result of changes to the eligibility criteria; and
  • the price of the Founding Groomer Onboarding Package will not increase for Groomers who have already paid.

We may change the standard sign-up incentive for Groomers who register in the future. Any such change will not affect Groomers already registered on the platform at the time the change is made.
