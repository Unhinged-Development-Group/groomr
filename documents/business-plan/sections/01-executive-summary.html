Executive Summary

Groomr is a UK-based online marketplace connecting dog owners with independent, local dog groomers. We exist to solve a genuine, frustrating, and surprisingly persistent problem: finding and booking a dog groomer in the UK is still a chaotic, fragmented process rooted in social media DMs and word-of-mouth — in 2026.

Our founder discovered this gap firsthand. Having moved several times across the UK with a dog in tow, the process of locating a new groomer meant trawling through Google results with no websites, hunting down Instagram profiles, and enduring back-and-forth messaging just to book an appointment. It is a poor customer experience that no modern service should accept.

Groomr changes that. We give pet owners a clean, intuitive platform to search for local groomers, view profiles with verified reviews and pricing, and book with confidence — complete with transparent deposit policies and instant confirmation. For groomers, we offer a professional digital presence and a dashboard to manage bookings, client records, messaging, and marketing without the chaos of Instagram Messenger.

The Opportunity

The UK professional dog grooming market was valued at over £420 million in 2022 and is forecast to grow at approximately 5.7% annually.[3] Of an estimated 10,600 groomers in the UK,[4] the vast majority operate independently with no formal online booking system. This is a significant and underserved opportunity.
