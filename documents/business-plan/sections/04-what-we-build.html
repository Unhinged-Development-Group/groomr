What We're Building

A dual-sided marketplace that feels invisible. We give both sides exactly what they need, wrapped in a beautiful, easy-to-use interface.

The Marketplace

Consumer Discovery App

The pet owner-facing product is a clean, mobile-first booking experience designed to take someone from 'I need a groomer' to 'appointment confirmed' in under five minutes.

Discovery

  • • Location-based search with distance, rating, and pricing surfaced upfront.
  • • Filters by service type, breed specialism, availability, and deposit policy.
  • • Rich groomer profile pages with photos, service menus, verified reviews, and credentials.

Booking

  • • Multi-step booking flow capturing dog details: name, breed, age, size, temperament, and health notes.
  • • Real-time availability calendar — no DMs, no phone calls required.
  • • Three-tier deposit policy surfaced at every stage: No Deposit / Fixed Deposit / Full Prepayment.
  • • Instant booking confirmation with automated reminders.

Trust & Safety

  • • Verified groomer profiles reviewed before going live.
  • • Transparent cancellation and refund terms displayed before booking.
  • • Post-appointment review system building a trustworthy signal layer over time.

The SaaS Layer

Groomer Operating System

The groomer-facing product is a professional dashboard replacing social media chaos with structured business management.

Bookings & Schedule

  • • Calendar view with today's schedule, upcoming bookings, and availability management.
  • • Booking table with status tracking, client name, dog details, and service booked.

Client Management

  • • Client profiles with full dog history, grooming notes, and private groomer observations.

Communication

  • • Integrated messaging with clients — no Facebook Messenger required.
  • • Automated appointment reminders reducing no-shows without any manual effort.

Marketing

  • • Mailing list with seasonal templates for client outreach.
  • • Campaign composition, recipient management, and engagement stats.

Financials & Analytics

  • • Detailed dashboard tracking real-time sales and bookings volume.
  • • Service performance tracking to clearly identify the most profitable offerings.
  • • Like-for-Like (LFL) reporting to compare current growth against historical data.
  • • Automated revenue projections and forecasting based on the upcoming scheduled book of business.

Business Settings

  • • Services, pricing, and availability managed in one place.
  • • Deposit policy selection: groomers choose what works for their business.
