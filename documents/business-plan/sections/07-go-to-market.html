Go-to-Market Strategy

Groomr's go-to-market strategy is deliberately local-first. Rather than a thin national launch, we build concentrated, high-quality presence in a small number of seed markets before expanding.

Phase 1: Seed Markets

Months 1–6

  • • Select 2–3 UK cities with high independent groomer density and active dog ownership.
  • • Founder-led direct groomer outreach: in-person visits, local grooming Facebook groups, industry forums.
  • • Founding Groomer Incentive: any groomer who signs up within the first 3 months receives zero commission for 6 months from their individual sign-up date. This is a time-limited, clearly communicated launch offer.
  • • Consumer-side demand seeded through targeted local digital advertising and dog owner community channels.

Phase 2: Regional Expansion

Months 6–18

  • • Expand to additional cities using seed market learnings.
  • • Introduce subscription tier for groomers who have seen tangible booking volume from the platform.
  • • Begin SEO investment to own 'dog groomer [city]' search terms nationally.
  • • Launch groomer referral incentives to accelerate supply-side growth.

Phase 3: National Scale

Month 18+

  • • National brand campaign to establish Groomr as the default dog grooming booking destination.
  • • Additional product features: breed-specific recommendations, mobile groomer listings, gift cards.
  • • Partnerships with pet insurance providers and veterinary chains for cross-referral.
