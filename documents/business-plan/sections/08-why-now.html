Why Now?

The conditions for Groomr's success have never been better aligned:

  • • The UK's dog population is at a historic high, and owner spending on professional care continues to climb.
  • • Social media is declining as a preferred communication channel for commercial transactions, particularly among 25–44 year olds — the core demographic of dog owners.
  • • The UK grooming sector is actively calling for professionalisation. A trusted booking platform that surfaces verified, professional groomers supports this goal.
  • • Consumer expectations around digital booking have been permanently reset by the pandemic. Waiting two days for a DM reply is no longer acceptable.
  • • No dominant player has yet established category ownership in UK dog grooming booking. The window to become the default platform is open — but not indefinitely.

Groomr is the right product, for the right market, at the right moment.
