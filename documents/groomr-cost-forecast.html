Infrastructure is 6.6% of total cost. Stripe is 93.4%. By Month 7–8 you're processing ~£180–210k/mo — enough to start a conversation with Stripe about custom rates. Negotiating to 1.2% + 20p would save roughly £2,400 over the forecast.

Watch Points

Stripe custom pricing — Month 7

Processing ~£178k/mo by Month 7. That's a reasonable threshold to open negotiations with Stripe for a custom rate.

Resend Pro — Month 5

2,520 bookings/mo in M5 means 2,520+ confirmation emails. Free tier caps at 3k/mo — you'll hit it around Month 5. Pro at £15.75/mo.

Vonage volume deal — Month 5–6

~100k SMS/month by Month 6. Worth contacting Vonage for a bundle — could bring per-message rate to £0.020–0.025.

Clerk → Pro (Month 8+)

Free to 10k MAU. With 80 groomers + growing owner base you may approach this in Month 8 or just after.

Notes

  • Ramp of 25/50/75/100% over 4 months is an estimate. Groomers with large existing client bases may ramp faster; brand new groomers may ramp slower. Adjust the matrix if you have better data.
  • £45 average booking assumed. At £40 avg: 8-month Stripe = £14,000. At £50 avg: £17,500.
  • Stripe 1.5% + £0.20 applies to UK Visa/Mastercard. Amex 2.2%, EU cards 2.5% + 20p — actual blended rate slightly higher.
  • SMS reminders assumed at 1 per booking. If you add a 24hr reminder AND a 1hr reminder per booking, double the Vonage figures.
  • USD services (Vercel, Supabase, Resend) fluctuate with GBP/USD. At £1 = $1.20 add ~£7/mo to fixed costs.
