Groomr Design System

The Iconography

Our icons are designed to be literal, recognizable, and warm. This document catalogs our library built with a consistent, expressive circular splash.

Design Guidelines

All icons must adhere to these foundational structural rules to feel like a cohesive family:

  • • Stroke Rules: Always use a stroke-width="1.5" with stroke-linecap="round" and stroke-linejoin="round" in Deep Slate.
  • • The Splash Shape: Always use a perfect circle. No exceptions.
  • • Size & Offset: The circle's radius and offset should vary organically per icon to add character, but must never be so large that it gets cut off by the canvas bounds.
  • • Splash Colors: Alternate dynamically between Groomr Gold (#eae45c) and Muted Terracotta (#c87964) at opacity="0.55".
