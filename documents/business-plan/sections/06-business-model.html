Business Model

Groomr generates revenue by facilitating high-trust connections. Our revenue is structured to scale directly with the success of the groomers on our platform.

Core Revenue Streams

Revenue Stream Strategy
All-Inclusive Commission (Year 1) Flat 8% fee on each new booking. Transparent, no hidden math, simple for the groomer.
Payment Processing Arbitrage Capturing the margin between gateway costs (e.g. Stripe 1.2%) and our all-inclusive rate. Invisible high-margin revenue.
Groomer Subscription (Year 2+) £14.99/mo premium tier for established groomers wanting advanced reporting & reduced 5% commissions.
Promotional Placements (Year 2+) Visibility boosts for groomers wanting to appear at the top of local search results.

Future Revenue Possibilities

As the platform scales into Phase 3 and beyond, our concentrated user base unlocks high-margin ancillary, affiliate, and referral opportunities within the broader pet care ecosystem:

Affiliate Commissions

% cut from retail and professional equipment sales (clippers, shampoos) made via a future dashboard shop.

Referral Bounties

Fixed lead-gen fees negotiated with pet insurance providers and vets for referring breed-specific customers.

Sponsored Content & Data

Direct-to-professional marketing fees and anonymised trend data for large pet supply brands.

Platform Share Philosophy

Groomr does not impose exclusivity on groomers. There is no contractual requirement to route all bookings through the platform. Instead, we earn platform share organically — by building a product so frictionless and valuable that groomers actively prefer it over manual booking management. Once a groomer sees Groomr handling reminders, deposits, client records, and confirmations automatically, managing bookings off-platform starts to feel like unnecessary extra work.

This approach builds trust and goodwill with the supply side at the critical onboarding stage, and avoids the adversarial dynamic that hard exclusivity clauses create in early-stage marketplace businesses.
