Prior Investment Declaration

01

Purpose of this document

This document is prepared by Groomr Ltd in response to HMRC's requirement that companies applying for SEIS Advance Assurance disclose all prior investment received, including the amounts, dates, and venture capital schemes under which any previous funding was obtained.

It further confirms that Groomr Ltd has not received investment through the Enterprise Investment Scheme (EIS) or from a Venture Capital Trust (VCT), either of which would disqualify the company from raising under SEIS.

02

SEIS eligibility confirmations

✕

No EIS investment received

Groomr Ltd has not received any investment under the Enterprise Investment Scheme at any time prior to this application.

✕

No VCT investment received

Groomr Ltd has not received any investment from a Venture Capital Trust at any time prior to this application.

✕

No prior SEIS investment

Groomr Ltd has not previously raised any amount under the Seed Enterprise Investment Scheme. This application represents the company's first SEIS raise.

✕

No institutional or third-party investment

Groomr Ltd has not received investment from any angel investor, institutional investor, accelerator, incubator, or grant-making body prior to this application.

03

Prior funding received

The only funding received by Groomr Ltd prior to this SEIS application is a modest founder contribution made by Andrew Hughes in his capacity as sole director and founder of the company. This represents personal funds introduced to cover early incorporation costs and pre-launch expenses.

Nature of founder contribution

The founder contribution is personal capital introduced by the director to cover costs incurred prior to the SEIS investment being in place — including incorporation fees, domain registration, and initial platform development costs. It is not an investment under any venture capital scheme and does not affect the company's eligibility for SEIS.

04

Confirmation of SEIS eligibility

On the basis of the above, Groomr Ltd confirms that it has not received any prior investment that would disqualify it from raising under the Seed Enterprise Investment Scheme. The company has not previously raised under EIS, received investment from a VCT, or raised any prior SEIS round.

Declaration

Groomr Ltd confirms that the information contained in this document is accurate and complete to the best of the directors' knowledge and belief. No investment has been received under the Enterprise Investment Scheme, from a Venture Capital Trust, or under any prior SEIS round. The only prior funding received is a modest personal contribution from the founder, which is not made under any venture capital scheme and does not affect the company's SEIS eligibility.
