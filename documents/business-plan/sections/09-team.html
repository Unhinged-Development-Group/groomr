Official Co-Pilot

Andrew brings a wealth of experience from hospitality and leisure management, where he specialized in aligning stakeholder needs to deliver exceptional service. A lifelong advocate for using technology to streamline processes and solve problems—a passion rooted in his early career in education—Andrew is now applying his expertise in operational efficiency to modernize the pet grooming industry.

Beside him is Murphy, a 5-year-old long-haired Chihuahua and the true inspiration behind Groomr. As CGO, Murphy maintains a strict daily nap schedule, enforces rigorous treat testing, and serves as a constant reminder of why a frictionless, trustworthy pet care experience matters.
